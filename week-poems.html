Poems for Yas

A little something to brighten every day of the week

Monday

Even Mondays are bright when you're around,
Your smile turns the week's first frown upside down.
The start of the week feels less like a chore,
Because you bring joy I can’t ignore.

The world may feel heavy, but you’re my light,
Turning the dull into something so bright.
With you, Monday’s magic is easy to see,
You’re the reason it’s special, at least to me.

Wednesday

Halfway through, the week feels long,
But you push forward, you stay so strong.
Even when the world may test,
You shine brighter than the rest.

Your smile’s a light, a midweek spark,
Guiding the way when the week feels dark.
So take a breath, you’re halfway there,
With all you’ve done, nothing compares.

Thursday

Thursday shines, a day so bright,
Because of you, it feels just right.
All week long, you’ve given your best,
Worked so hard, stood out from the rest.

Your beauty glows, your strength inspires,
You light the way, igniting fires.
Thursday’s glow is a gift you share,
A reminder of how much you care.

So pause, take pride in the path you pave,
You’re beautiful, brilliant, and endlessly brave.

Saturday

Saturday beams with endless light,
A day that feels so pure, so right.
The week is done, and you can rest,
And let your heart feel at its best.

With every laugh, with every smile,
You make the world feel worthwhile.
A day for dreams, for all that’s new,
Saturday shines because of you.

Sunday

Sundays remind me how lucky I am,
To know your heart, to hold your hand.
The peace you bring when we talk or just stay,
Makes every moment feel like a warm ray.

There’s no one else who could hold my heart,
You’ve had it from the very start.
In your presence, the world feels whole,
You’ve become the rhythm that soothes my soul.

You mean so much, more than words can say,
My heart is yours every step of the way.
