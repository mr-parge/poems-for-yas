For Yas

Yas, you light up my days,
In your presence, I’m amazed.
Your strength, your mind, your sweet embrace,
Forever, you’re my favorite place.

My Christmas Angel

This Christmas day, I watched in awe,
Your strength, your grace, without a flaw.
Amid the rush, the endless tide,
You held your kindness deep inside.

Through every smile, each gentle word,
Your care was felt, your heart was heard.
The world may have asked so much of you,
But you gave your all and stayed so true.

And even then, you thought of me,
A love so rare, like destiny.
The medicine you sent my way,
Showed love that words could never say.

You are my world, my light, my star,
My heart’s safe place, just as you are.
Tonight, as you rest, please always know,
I’m proud of you more than I can show.

You’re my forever, my soul’s sweet song,
The love I’ve searched for all along.
My Christmas angel, in all you do,
Your beauty and strength always shine through.
